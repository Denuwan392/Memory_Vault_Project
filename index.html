Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account or log in?

No! The Google Form doesn't require any login. You can upload anonymously.

Can other students see my uploads?

Absolutely not. Only the official media team can access the uploaded files. Your privacy is our priority.

What if I have more than 10 files?

You can submit the form multiple times! Just click the upload button again after your first submission.

How will my photos/videos be used?

Your submissions will be used to create historical archives, anniversary albums, and school publications. We'll always credit contributors when possible.
